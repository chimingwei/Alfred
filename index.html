Help Page

Clicking the left most icon brings up the Google Calender Information. Clicking the Second icon brings up this help page. Clicking the third icon brings up the Weather Data. Clicking the fourth or fifth icons bring up the Facebook feed. To close a window press the icon a second time using buttons or touchscreen.
Google Calender Data is accurate within the last rhity minutes. All Weather Data has been collected by the Weather Box and has been updated within the past minute. If the Weather Box is not in range of is turned off, the weather data displayed is the last known data.
